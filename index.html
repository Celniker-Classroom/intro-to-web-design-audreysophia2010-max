My Early Life:

I was born in Seattle, Washington, in 2010, on a small landlocked island called Mercer Island. I was the middle child in a family of five: My twin sister was two minutes younger, and my older brother was 6 years older. Growing up, I always spent all my time with my sister since she was my best friend, and we used to share a bunk bed. In my free time, I liked reading, watching TV, and playing sports like soccer or basketball. In 3rd grade, I moved to a new elementary school called Solana Ranch and met all of the people who are now my closest friends. I then went to PTMS for middle school and am currently at CCA High School. Seattle

My Current Interests:

One of my favorite pastimes is playing sports. I play field hockey in the fall, soccer in the winter, and track in the spring. I love sports because I get to meet many new people and do exercise, but most of all, because I get to spend time with my friends. A lot of my closest friends are people I’ve met in sports. In my free time, I also enjoy baking and cooking. One of the most memorable things I’ve cooked is when my friends and I made homemade pasta with bolognese sauce.
